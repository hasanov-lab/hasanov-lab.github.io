June 11, 2026 Research

Mostafa Presents Work on RCC Brain Metastases

Proud to see our PhD student, Mostafa, present his work on the single-cell and spatial landscape of renal cell carcinoma brain metastases.

Using single-nucleus RNA-seq, spatial transcriptomics, and computational biology, this research explores mechanisms of immune dysfunction and therapeutic resistance in CNS metastases.

This work advances our understanding of brain metastasis biology and precision oncology.
