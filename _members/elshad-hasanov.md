Dr. Elshad Hasanov is a physician-scientist and Assistant Professor of Medical Oncology at The Ohio State University Comprehensive Cancer Center (OSUCCC – James). His laboratory focuses on genitourinary malignancies, renal cell carcinoma (RCC), and pan-cancer brain metastasis. His research integrates single-cell omics, tumor immunology, and computational approaches to understand the tumor microenvironment and develop novel immunotherapy strategies.
